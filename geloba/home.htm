The V International Meeting on Lorentzian Geometry (GeLoBa2009) will take place at Hotel Villa Rosa in Martina Franca (Taranto, Italy) from July 8th to July 11th, 2009.

In keeping with the spirit of the previous conferences held in Spain (Lorentzian Geometry. Benalmádena 2001, Geometría de Lorentz. Murcia 2003, III International Meeting on Lorentzian Geometry, Castelldefels 2005 and IV International Meeting on Lorentzian Geometry. Santiago de Compostela 2007) the conference will focus on Mathematical Relativity and Lorentzian Geometry. It will be divided into mini-courses and lectures given by outstanding specialists. There will also be short communications and poster sessions.
